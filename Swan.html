JOSETTE SWAN


STREAMER | COSPLAYER






One of Malaysia's fastest rising esports and
gaming influencers, Josette got her start
streaming horror games on Twitch. Since then,
the self-proclaimed goth gamer girl has made
a name for herself in the cosplaying scene as
well.

She also works as an ambassadress for brands
such as Asus ROG and HyperX. Whether she's
giving off the cute girl aura many adore her for,
or the mischievous, persona she has in-game,
Josette is a consummate professional who means
business.
