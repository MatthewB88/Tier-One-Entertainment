JAZ "HENERAL TUNA" COMODA


SHOUTCASTER






If you're a fan of pinoy Dota 2 then chances are
you've heard of Jaz Comoda. The General Santos
City native was one of the first talents to be put
a through the Tier One system and in one short
year has emerged as one of the most exciting
new voices in the local shoutcasting scene.

More than the suave mustache that makes him look
like an actual Philippine general, Heneral Tuna
wages war against all those who would support the
"peenoise" stereotype. With his quest to better himself
and the Dota community around him, Jaz is poised to
take the local Dota 2 scene into a bright future.
