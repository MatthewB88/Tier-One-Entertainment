FOUNDING OF TIER ONE ENTERTAINMENT


In the digital era of advertising, those who don't take gaming seriously will be left behind. - TRYKE GUTIERREZ, CEO OF TIER ONE ENTERTAINMENT

Tier One Entertainment was founded in 2017 by esports veteran Tryke Gutierrez and cosplay and gaming star Alodia Gosiengfiao. Together, they sought to revolutionize the esports landscape in Southeast Asia.

By focusing on the development of truly talented, top-caliber personalities, Tier One’s mission is to connect brands with the unique gaming audience like never before.

Tryke Gutierrez is locally known as one of the first two shoutcasters in the country. He is also known for organizing numerous events and one of the pioneers of Philippine eSports. He has numerous experience in eSports from shoutcasting, event organizing, eSports publications, influencer management, marketing thru eSports, managing professional eSports teams, and more. He was once a competitive player that started out organizing events due to the lack of opportunities for tournaments in the region.
Alodia Gosiengfiao is an international cosplay icon and has been guesting and judging in different cosplay conventions around the world for the past few years. She has also been featured in various magazines, newspapers, and TV shows locally and abroad. She was named by UNO Magazine as one of the Most Influential Women in the Philippines. She was also named as one of the most popular Cosplayers in the world according to TheRichest.com
Commanding the helm of two major Philippine companies at a young age, Brian Lim is no stranger to managing high-profile projects. What sets him apart aside from his business acumen is his love for esports, serving as Philippine eSports Organization's chairman in 2012.


SUPPORTED BY BITKRAFT ESPORTS VENTURES
BITKRAFT is an early stage investor and company builder focussed entirely on the esports sector. They find, support, and scale esports startups along with the industry’s most influential companies.
